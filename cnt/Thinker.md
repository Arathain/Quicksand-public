Mass-produced replica of a refurbished [[Filacter]] unit, sold to NCI by an anonymous [[Executive]]. Having high computational speed and boasting an impressive memory capacity, it exceeds in ranged hacking attacks.

Main gameplay loop involves a hacking minigame, wherein parts of the enemy's consciousness have to be matched with your own [[Data Chips]]' space. At any point, Thinker is able to unleash an ultimate attack that deals percentage damage to him at a fixed rate, and to the enemy based off of how many parts he holds. Thinker gains shields upon 'perfect clearing' a given hacking minigame, allowing him to mitigate *some* of the damage inflicted upon him.


Pronounced "Sinker".

Character story revolves around 'feeling as if "you" aren't your own', spiralling doubt, and a fear of losing oneself. Thinker has mixed romantic feelings for his assigned [[Executive]] (male), and a deeply scarred psyche regarding his 'childhood' and service under his "master's" family.
