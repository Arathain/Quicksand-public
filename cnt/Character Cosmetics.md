#character

There are three layers to character cosmetic options:
- **Platings**, which are used to apply patterns/colours to parts of a character (see: ACVI part colours, Titanfall 2 sprays)
	- Platings are separated into **textures**, **gradients**, and **colours**.
		- **Textures** can be applied to any texture with UV coordinates; this will usually be smaller plates and isolated parts, seeing as we don't want continuous patterns to get too tiring or visually jarring.
		- **Gradients** can, on top of regular parts, also be applied to components that might find it harder to have a clean UV mapping, and will instead be one-dimensional - hair, slim parts, fingers, etc. fit this category
		- Solid **colours** can be applied to all parts.
	- There is a hard limit of 6 (TBD based off of technical limitations, e.g. texture size) platings per character - the design should fit under these constraints when thinking about customisation.
- **Decals**, which can be placed on specific parts of a character's outfit
- **Stylings**, which are model changes such as hairstyle and outfit - **this category of cosmetic can be monetised**, but is not part of a Gachapon system.
