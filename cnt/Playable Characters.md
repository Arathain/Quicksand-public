Playable Characters are categorised into three classes:
[[Heavy Type]] units, which get *into* the enemy's damage cone and manually block, clash, and parry attacks
[[Light Type]] units, which closely avoid the enemy's damage cone to unleash high damage from the sides
and [[Artillery Type]] units, which derive their difficulty from other areas (strategy, aim-based gameplay, rhythm games, etc.)

A character's kit is defined by [[Weapon Cores]], [[Data Chips]], and Surplus Units (Character Copies) - There is no character levelling system; it is instead partially supplanted by a per-character [[Ranking System]].
