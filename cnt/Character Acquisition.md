#mechanic #monetisation

Apart from the starter units, characters are acquired through three routes:
- **Gachapon**: a randomised, low-stakes option, that can be saved once purchased (maximum of 6)
- **Direct Purchase**: available directly on release, and not costing much more than getting a random character, but is restricted outside of sale event windows - for those who want to get a specific character on-release, or when they come back into rotation.
- **Anniversary Order:** refreshed (once/twice) every year, provides a customised "gachapon" guaranteed to include the player's character of choice - this is a safety measure for any character going out of direct purchase for too long, as well as new players coming to the game only to find that their favourite is currently unavailable.

All purchase options are accessed through the player's home menu, with the player's available gachapon machines being physically placed in the 3D space of the player quarters, up to 6.
