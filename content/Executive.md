#story 

A person descended from a Corp leader; allowed to speak Old Common, and generally considered politically and practically immune. Being able to afford complex and costly procedures in order to regularly revitalise themselves, they can live forever, with the only limit being the confines of their memory - thus, most undergo the procedure of offsetting their excess memories inside of a [[Filacter]], which acts as their 'second brain' and third hand.

Quietly exempt from being used as a basis for MAs.
