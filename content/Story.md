[[World Overview - Dictionary]]

The game's story is told on two fronts - the geopolitical, "global" side, and the relationship between the player and their characters. The former works on a 'per-patch' basis, while the latter is progressed as the player and the characters interact - usually, the global story will affect the local, rather than vice versa.
