Ethos: A rejection of humanity as a coping mechanism for perpetrating and being subjected to horrible crimes.

The second (wow that's crazy) unit in the Lance Pillar's (Verilanza) debut MA line. The root person the MA originates from was a Subject Guard officer, and participated in numerous crimes under the orders of a Verilanza-controlled territory. Model Two attempts to reject their humanity, and appears a misanthrope, but comes to terms with their 'human' self as their relationship with the player progresses, and they accept that the range of what is 'humanity' extends beyond its best and its worst.
