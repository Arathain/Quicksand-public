#story 

Administrative Regency Mandate (ARM) - the greatest political force in the known universe, formed after the fallout of the Final War; works "in tandem" with the numerous Pillars around the universe.

Magisterium - the judicial and executive core of the ARM - a local branch of the Magisterium has complete control and final verdict over all ARM forces in the region.

Pillars - inter-stellar megacorporations, "friendly accomplices" of the Administrative Regency Mandate

Mirrored-Actor (MA) - a digitised copy of an existing consciousness; generally used to replace "true AI", owing to the complexity of having to create a brand-new consciousness. MA technology tends to "entangle" the digitised copy with the original, causing death or other negative events of the original being to have lasting impact on the copies.

[[Executive]] - An upper-classman, effectively nobility with complete economic and political impunity.

[[Tone]] - A microorganism capable of transmitting and processing data nigh-instantly.

The Shrive - a semi-cataclysmic event that fractured causality on a cosmic scale, and allow for human will to shape reality. Done as a last resort by the losing side of the Final War, it has allowed for the existences of Mages, instant travel, and instant transmission of information via connecting to conceptual 'spheres of mindscape'. The details of this event are a tightly-kept secret by the elite of the ARM.

Sorcerer/Sourcerer/Sourcer - anyone deeply instructed in the ability to interface with technology/'magic'; mages, diviners, technicians. Unsanctioned Sorcery is strictly prohibited by the Pillars and the ARM.

[[Mage]] - Persons able to manipulate the world via usage of enforced pragmas; such talents have become possible ever since the Shrive, but are difficult to come by for most.

Diviner - persons skilled in observing channel activity and interstellar 'currents', using such metrics to predict the happenings of powers that be.
