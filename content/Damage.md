#mechanic 

Damage consists of a flat value and a health-scaling coefficient - the latter should generally be used sparingly. Said damage can have any number of 'tags' or 'types' applied to it, which in turn may store extra data in the damage's field. 

Said types include:

- **Crush** - "Blunt" attack type; generally stronger against armoured opponents, but less able to take advantage of critical weaknesses
- **Slash** - a fairly common attack type, inflicting shallow but grazing wounds; extra effective on Critical [[Toughness]] zones.
- **Pierce** - an 'all-rounder' damage type, standing at a midpoint between Slash and Crush; 
- **Erode** - a damage type generally associated with DoT (Damage over Time), referring to corrosion / friction / sanding. Might be used in combination with another of the above damage types to elaborate on the type of over-time damage being dealt.

All serving as 'physical' damage types, and generally kept mutually exclusive, as well as:

- **Thermal Damage**
	- **Overheat**
	- **Freeze**
- **Resonate
- **Obliterate (True Damage)**

Which are 'special' damage types that can cause unique interactions.
