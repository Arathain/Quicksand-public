#mechanic

Enemies (and enemy parts) have varying levels of 'toughness', that determine what happens when they are hit with [[Damage]] from an attack:

- ### Counterkinetic
	-  'Reflects' a set percentage of damage dealt; does not deal damage, but players will want to avoid hitting this.
	-  Guaranteed to trigger a [[Bounce]] if the attack is melee.
- ### Armoured
	-  Provides per-damage-type defenses, just like Normal toughness
	-  However, it deals 0.75x times less damage than Normal toughness, and triggers a [[Bounce]] more easily by providing a 1.5x multiplier to the threshold.
- ### Normal
	-  Default state; provides per-damage-type defenses.
- ### Critical
	-  Provides per-damage-type defenses, just like Normal toughness; however, it provides a 1.25x multiplier to all damage dealt.
	-  Attacks will never bounce when hitting Critical Toughness parts.
