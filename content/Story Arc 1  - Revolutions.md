#revolutions 

(Title has O's styled as spinning/revolving)

Musical theming for the locale:
- blend of Entertainment Hardcore, 200step, and polka/**modern** eeu folk music
- use of accordion and other eastern european folk instruments

A planet conquered by an independent, Anti-ARM state, the [[Materiya Sovereignty]]; a totalitarian state composed of a central region and a host of 'lesser', conquered vassal states. The storyline focuses on historical reflections of relocations, ethnic cleansing / genocide (both that of humans and of cultures), and the terror of an occupied state. The low-level reality of the world is explored by a once-refugee of the planet, now journalist, seeking to document and shed light upon the happenings within the would-be empire.

# Synopsis

The player faction is initially sent to stoke fire in the Sovereignty via a series of (what is later revealed to be, but can already be deduced as) false flag operations, but are soon presented an offer by a liberation group of one of the vassal states, and seize the opportunity.
