#mechanic 

Data Chips contain various skills and character effects, which are used to enhance character performance, or change their playstyle to various degrees. The total 'power' of a data chip is quantified as an integer "Computational Load" (CL).

Data Chips are a layered packing-problem system of progression - characters have both a limited amount of 'chip slots', each with a limited size, and a limited 'data storage space', which is a 3D matrix of grid cells; A data chip occupies space in both the chip slot and 'DSS' space. The chip slot is a simple size ordinal; e.g. 1-cost, 2-cost, 3-cost, 4-cost. Meanwhile, the DSS space taken up by the chip is determined by its CL, and is grown into a random voxel shape.
